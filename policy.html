Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process and protect your Personal Information. Please read this policy carefully.

Clicks Group Limited ("CGL", "we", "us", "our") is a health, beauty and wellness retailer operating in South Africa, Namibia, Botswana, eSwatini and Lesotho.

Our head office is located at Cnr Searle and Pontac Street, Woodstock, Cape Town, South Africa.

Personal Information We Process

Personal Information refers to information about an identifiable person.

  • Identity Information (name, ID number, gender, date of birth)
  • Contact Information (email, phone number, address)
  • Healthcare Information (pharmacy and medical data)
  • Financial Information (payment details)
  • Transaction Information (purchase history)
  • Technical Information (IP address, browser type)
  • Loyalty Information
  • Marketing Preferences
  • CCTV footage in stores

How We Collect Your Information

  • Directly from you when you purchase products or book services
  • Automatically when you use our website or mobile app
  • From third parties when necessary to provide services

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide services and loyalty rewards
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To improve website functionality
  • To send relevant marketing communications
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your Personal Information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers and suppliers
  • Regulatory authorities where required by law
  • Affinity and loyalty programme partners

Storage & Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your Personal Information. We may transfer information outside South Africa but ensure adequate protection.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your Personal Information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion where applicable
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent

Children

We process Personal Information relating to children only where legally required or with consent from a competent person.

Direct Marketing

You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by updating your preferences or contacting us.
