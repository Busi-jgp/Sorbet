About Sorbet Cape Town CBD

Welcome to Sorbet Cape Town CBD! We are a modern, inclusive beauty and wellness destination for everyone. Our mission is to help you look and feel your best in a friendly, professional environment. Whether you’re here for a haircut, a relaxing facial , or expert grooming, our team is dedicated to providing top-quality service with a personal touch.

Your premier destination for beauty and wellness services. Experience luxury and relaxation with every visit.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional beauty and wellness experiences for all, using the latest techniques and the best products. We believe self-care is for everyone, and we strive to make every guest feel welcome and valued.

Our Vision

To be Cape Town’s most trusted and inclusive beauty brand, known for our vibrant atmosphere, expert care, and commitment to every client’s unique needs. We want every visit to be a positive, uplifting experience.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: Welcoming all genders, ages, and backgrounds
  • Quality: Using top products and modern techniques
  • Care: Personalized attention for every guest
  • Community: Supporting local wellness and empowerment

Meet Our Team

Our friendly team includes stylists, therapists, and wellness experts, all passionate about helping you shine. We regularly update our skills and stay on top of the latest trends to serve you better. Come visit us and experience the Sorbet difference!
